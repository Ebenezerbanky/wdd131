Lens & Light Portfolio

This name was chosen to evoke the fundamental elements of photography - the lens that captures the image and the light that makes photography possible. It's memorable, professional, and directly communicates the site's purpose as a photography portfolio.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a professional photography portfolio to:

  • Showcase diverse photography work across multiple categories (landscape, portrait, street photography)
  • Provide potential clients with information about my photography style and experience
  • Facilitate contact and booking inquiries for photography services
  • Display technical proficiency through responsive galleries and interactive features

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "I'm planning my wedding and looking for a photographer. Can I see examples of your portrait and event photography work, and how can I contact you for availability and pricing?"

Scenario 2: "I'm an art director interested in your street photography style. Where can I find your most recent work in this category?"

Color Schema

Primary Color (Dark Gray - #2d3436): Used for main text and headings

Secondary Color (Medium Gray - #636e72): Used for navigation and secondary text

Accent Color (Light Gray - #b2bec3): Used for borders, highlights, and hover states

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display - An elegant serif font that adds sophistication to titles and headings

Body Text: Raleway - A clean, modern sans-serif font that ensures excellent readability while complementing the imagery
